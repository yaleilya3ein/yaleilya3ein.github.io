Top 5 ( #top5 )

Frequently, Yaleil Ya3ein formulates a top 5 list. The list is non-biased and based on post-performance only.

Fan of the Week ( #fanoftheweek )

None of this is possible if not for the constant viewership and support from the audience. Therefore Yaleil Ya3ein has dedicated a post for appreciating the fans based on their interaction performance.

Youtube Featured ( #youtubefeatured )

YouTube Featured is a show dedicated to branch the Artist’s reach to an even wider audience. By sharing snippets of their YouTube videos, we hope to increase the diversity of viewership and enhance engagement. To be featured on this show please use the following hashtag: #youtubefeatured.

Original Audio ( #orginalaudio )

Original Audio is a show dedicated to sharing the Artist’s musical examples outside of Instagram. It is important to engage the community outside instagram, where the community will be then aware of the hidden content of each Artist.

Cover Competitions ( #cover_artist )

Cover Competitions are done when Yaleil Ya3ein collaborates with a well-established artist. In this program we ask our community members to cover a new song that is released by the well-established artist for a chance to be featured and appreciated on his/her channel. Recent work includes a collaboration with the super star Yousef Arafat in covering his latest song Ma’a Al Salama.To see examples please search the following hashtag: #coveryousef
